### US-06 Current Acceptance Rules

- Every team requires its own subscription, including the first team.
- Users choose 39 RUB for 30 days or 390 RUB for 365 days.
- Checkout is an explicit educational mock: no real payment provider, charge, card data, or payment credentials are used.
- Users can view expiry dates and enable or disable mock auto-renewal separately for each team.
