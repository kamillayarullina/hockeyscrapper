🔥 Problem

  • 🏟️ Ticket announcements are spread across different club websites
  • ⚡ Sales may open unexpectedly
  • 🔄 Fans have to manually check multiple sources
  • ⏳ Ticket sales are sometimes announced after they have already started

💡 Solution

HockeyScrapper

Automatically monitors ticket sales information and notifies users when tickets become available for their favourite teams.
